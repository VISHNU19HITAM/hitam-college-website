Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)

About

The CSE program builds strong fundamentals in programming, system design and computational theory. Students gain hands-on experience through labs, projects and industry internships.

Future

Graduates are well placed for careers in software, cloud computing, cybersecurity and research; growth in AI/ML and distributed systems keeps demand high.

Achievements

  • Consistent high placement rates with graduates joining top tech firms.
  • Student projects have won regional hackathons and research showcases.
  • Active industry partnerships for internships and live projects.

CSE — Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

About

This specialization focuses on machine learning algorithms, deep learning frameworks and practical AI applications across industries.

Future

Strong demand in sectors like healthcare, finance and autonomous systems; graduates pursue roles as ML engineers, research interns and AI consultants.

Achievements

  • Faculty-led projects published in conferences and journals.
  • Students placed in AI-focused roles and internships at startups and MNCs.
  • Regular AI workshops and guest lectures from industry experts.

CSE — Data Science

About

The Data Science track teaches statistical modeling, ETL pipelines and visualization techniques to extract insights from large datasets.

Future

Data-driven decision making across industries creates roles in analytics, data engineering and product analytics; skills in ML and cloud data stacks are valuable.

Achievements

  • Alumni working as data scientists and analytics leads in product companies.
  • Department hosts data challenges and collaborates with industry sponsors for capstone projects.

Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

About

ECE blends circuit theory, signal processing and embedded systems design to enable careers in telecom, hardware and IoT product development.

Future

Emerging technologies like 5G, IoT and edge computing drive demand for ECE graduates in R&D and product development roles.

Achievements

  • Students have built award-winning embedded projects and IoT prototypes.
  • Collaborations with local industries for training and internships.

Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE)

About

EEE focuses on power systems, control theory and power electronics to prepare students for roles in energy, automation and industrial systems.

Future

With growing emphasis on renewable energy and smart grids, EEE graduates are positioned for opportunities in energy transition and automation.

Achievements

  • Faculty and students contribute to regional energy projects and workshops.
  • Hands-on labs and industry tie-ups enhance practical learning.

Mechanical Engineering

About

Mechanical engineering covers design, manufacturing processes and thermofluids with emphasis on CAD, prototyping and industrial applications.

Future

Demand exists in manufacturing, automotive, aerospace and product design; additive manufacturing and automation broaden career paths.

Achievements

  • Student teams participate in design competitions and prototyping challenges.
  • Industry-sponsored projects provide real-world exposure.

Civil Engineering

About

Civil engineering trains students in structural analysis, construction materials and project management for infrastructure development.

Future

Opportunities in urban infrastructure, sustainable construction and project management continue to grow with increasing development projects.

Achievements

  • Alumni working on major infrastructure and consultancy projects.
  • Student projects in sustainable construction and water management.
