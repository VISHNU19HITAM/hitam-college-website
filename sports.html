About

HITAM encourages physical fitness and teamwork through cricket, football, basketball, badminton and athletics. Regular inter-college tournaments and coaching camps are organised.

Facilities

  • Full-size football/cricket ground
  • Indoor badminton and basketball courts
  • Gymnasium and fitness centre

Achievements

  • Multiple inter-college championship wins in regional tournaments.
  • Alumni represented state-level teams.
